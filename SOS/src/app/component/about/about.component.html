"Search Online Supervisor" is a web based application, which is designed for the students, who are facing difficulties in searching their own supervisors and examiners.

Here, the students who have been registered can search whether the supervisors from the same domain are available or not and can send a request to them.

This web application can help the students by reducing their efforts and time in searching for a supervisor, and it makes the process easy and flexible.
